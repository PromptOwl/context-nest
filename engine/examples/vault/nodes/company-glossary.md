# Company Glossary

Common terms and acronyms used at Acme Corp.

## Business Terms

**ARR** (Annual Recurring Revenue)
- Total yearly value of active subscriptions
- Key metric for SaaS growth

**CAC** (Customer Acquisition Cost)
- Total sales/marketing spend ÷ new customers
- Target: < $5,000 per customer

**Churn Rate**
- % of customers lost in a period
- Target: < 5% monthly

**MRR** (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
- Predictable monthly subscription income
- Excludes one-time fees

**NPS** (Net Promoter Score)
- Customer satisfaction metric
- Scale: -100 to +100

## Technical Terms

**API** (Application Programming Interface)
- Way for systems to communicate
- See [[API Documentation]]

**CI/CD** (Continuous Integration/Deployment)
- Automated build and release pipeline
- Uses GitHub Actions

**K8s** (Kubernetes)
- Container orchestration platform
- Manages our production workloads

**Observability**
- Monitoring, logging, and tracing
- Stack: Datadog + Splunk

**SEV** (Service Event)
- Production incident requiring response
- See [[SEV Management Runbook]]

## Organizational

**DRI** (Directly Responsible Individual)
- Person accountable for outcome
- Every project has one DRI

**IC** (Individual Contributor)
- Non-management role
- Levels: IC1 through IC6

**OKR** (Objectives & Key Results)
- Goal-setting framework
- Quarterly planning cycle

**Retro** (Retrospective)
- Team reflection meeting
- Held after sprints or incidents

## Product Terms

**Beta**
- Pre-release testing phase
- Limited user access

**GA** (General Availability)
- Publicly released version
- Fully supported

**MVP** (Minimum Viable Product)
- Simplest version that provides value
- Ships fast, iterates quickly

**POC** (Proof of Concept)
- Small experiment to validate idea
- Not production-ready

**Roadmap**
- Planned features over time
- Updated quarterly

## People & Culture

**Buddy System**
- New hire paired with experienced employee
- 90-day mentorship

**OOO** (Out of Office)
- Not available/working
- Update Slack status

**PTO** (Paid Time Off)
- Vacation and sick leave
- Unlimited policy

**Skip Level**
- Meeting with manager's manager
- Quarterly feedback opportunity

## Tools & Systems

**BambooHR**
- HRIS (HR Information System)
- Time off requests, org chart

**Jira**
- Project management
- Bug and feature tracking

**Linear**
- Alternative to Jira
- Used by engineering teams

**Notion**
- Internal documentation
- Team wikis and processes

**Slack**
- Team communication
- Primary chat platform

## Acronyms

- **AMA**: Ask Me Anything
- **ASAP**: As Soon As Possible
- **ETA**: Estimated Time of Arrival
- **FYI**: For Your Information
- **IMO/IMHO**: In My (Humble) Opinion
- **LMK**: Let Me Know
- **OOO**: Out of Office
- **POC**: Point of Contact (or Proof of Concept)
- **TBD**: To Be Determined
- **TL;DR**: Too Long; Didn't Read
- **WFH**: Work From Home
