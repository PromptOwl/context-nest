# Your AI Doesn't Know Your Business. ContextNest Fixes That.

Every company has tribal knowledge trapped in docs, runbooks, glossaries, and Slack threads. Your AI assistant? It's flying blind without it.

**ContextNest is a structured knowledge vault that gives AI the context it needs to actually be useful** — not generic, not hallucinated, but grounded in *your* company's reality.

## What Goes In the Vault

Everything your team already knows but your AI doesn't: API docs, brand guidelines, deployment workflows, incident runbooks, onboarding guides, engineering values, compliance policies, even code snippets. Typed, tagged, and scoped so the right context surfaces at the right time.

## Why It Matters

Most enterprise AI fails not because the model is bad, but because the context is missing. ContextNest sits between your team's knowledge and your AI layer — a persistent, queryable memory that turns a general-purpose model into *your* model.

- **No fine-tuning required.** Just structured markdown and smart retrieval.
- **Scoped access.** Public, team, or restricted — your data stays where it should.
- **MCP-native.** Plugs directly into any Model Context Protocol workflow.
- **Built for domain experts.** No code, no CLI, no friction.

## From Generic to Governed

A support bot that knows your product glossary. A dev assistant that follows your deployment process. An onboarding copilot that sounds like your culture, not a template. That's what happens when AI has real context.

**ContextNest. The knowledge layer your AI has been missing.**
