# Welcome to Acme Corp!

We're excited to have you join our team. This guide will help you get started.

## Your First Week

### Day 1: Setup
- [ ] Collect laptop and badge from IT
- [ ] Complete HR paperwork
- [ ] Set up email and Slack
- [ ] Join #new-hires channel

### Day 2: Orientation
- [ ] Company overview presentation
- [ ] Meet your team
- [ ] Review org chart
- [ ] Set up 1:1 with manager

### Day 3-5: Getting Started
- [ ] Access required systems
- [ ] Review team documentation
- [ ] Shadow team members
- [ ] Start first project

- Your Manager: Schedule weekly 1:1s
- Your Buddy: Assigned on day 1

## Common Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Bi-weekly on Fridays. See [[Benefits Overview]] for details.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Use the PTO system in BambooHR. Notify your manager first.

**Q: What's the dress code?**
A: Casual. Wear what makes you comfortable.
